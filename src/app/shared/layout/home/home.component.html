Eco
Give us your budget for transportation, we will try to find a solution through ground or air partial loading.
Budget is an economic service where the lowest possible price is a priority, in the case of air transport, the transit time is within 14 days from dispatch. In the case of land transport, we will try to find capacity nearby, thereby saving costs and the environment in order to maximize the utilization of the given means of transport.

FAQ
What is your prefered mean of communication?
At first there are three means of communication – online chat, email and call. For order/quote related communication e-mail is essential, while phone call is the fastest mean of communication. In case you dislike anything, do not hesitate to give us a call so we can find a address your feedback. As foretold – feedback is mother of partnership and mutually beneficient cooperation.
Can I ship all around the world with WE-DEL?
Well, almost yes! Of course there are some exceptions. We can’t offer shipment to: Cuba, North Korea, Iran, Syria, Belarus, Sudan, South Sudan, Yemen, Somalia, Burma (Maymar) and of course to Russia. At WE-DEL we believe that the firm spine is more important then profit. Additional check-ups may apply to the shipment that raise a suspicion that might be transfered to Russia through various transit countries.
Which shipments is WE-DEL able to transfer?
WE-DEL is able to transfer everything via Air/ground transport. Our service is mostly designed for time-critical shipments and cost-effective shipments above 70 kg. But no worries – we can also provide you transport or smaller parcels via our courier partners for competitive prices.
Are you working during holidays/weekends?
At WE-DEL ve value your time a lot. Therefore you can approach as anytime via phone call. At our regular work hours, which is from Monday to Friday 08-17 CET we will try our utmost best tol reply you within one hour from sending of mail infromation.

For OnTime service you can give us a call 24/7/365.
Is my shipment insured against any damage/losage of cargo?
As a freight forwarding company the cargo you are shipping with us is governed by insurance of our carrier. With that said it is by Montreal & Warszaw agreement in the case of air transport and by CMR in case of ground transport. On top of it WE-DEL has insurance with limit 3 000 000 CZK for freight-forwarding related mistakes. If your shipment exceeds this amouth, do not hesitate to write us for additional insurance.
How can I track my shipment?
We are having tracking option on the page. If for any reason the tracking is not giving updated informations we are always awailable to check the shipment progress via phone/mail/online chat. As for our premium service OnTime we are offering 24/7 tracking.
