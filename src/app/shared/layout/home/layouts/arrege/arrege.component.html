Air Import & Export
Our signature service? Air transportation. If you order an import service, we will arrange the transport through our network of local expert partners proven over years which are operating in accordance with IATA and WCA. We will hapilly handle export shipments from our Prague office or different europian airports.

We can offer transportation in following modes:
  • Door to door,
  • Door to airport,
  • Airport to airport,
  • Airport to door,
  • Air charter (OnTime),
  • On Board Courier (OnTime)
Ground Transportation
Land transport goes hand in hand with air transport. We can offer both a dedicated vehicle for time-sensitive shipments, as well as cost-optimal loading that saves costs and the environment.
We carry out ground transportation exclusively through verified partners with sufficient insurance coverage.
Dangerous Goods
At WE-DEL we are certified dangerous goods experts certified by the International Aviation Association IATA.
We will be happy to arrange for you preparation of shipment according to the IATA Dangerous goods regulation and then transport it to your desired location.
